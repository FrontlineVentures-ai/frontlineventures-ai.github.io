About

Pattern recognition
from first principles.

Tim House held senior advisory roles at BDO and ICG, working with corporate clients on ESG strategy and sustainability across multiple sectors. Before moving into consulting, he served at UN Headquarters in New York, coordinating international peacekeeping operations spanning multiple theatres and coalition partners.

His earlier career in the British Army included operational tours in Afghanistan, senior command and staff appointments, and he was one of the lead negotiators for an Anglo-Canadian defence treaty. He has also served as a Non-Executive Director of the Global Bioethics Initiative, a New York-based international organisation.

Frontline Ventures operates at the intersection of strategic advisory and applied AI — building intelligence systems and market growth programmes for SMEs and Tier 2–3 suppliers with serious ambitions in UK and North American markets.

The firm's competitive advantage is not the technology alone. It is the ability to construct the right question, point the system at the right problem, and translate the output into a decision.
